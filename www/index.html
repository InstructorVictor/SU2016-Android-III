Learn Art

ART101

Introduction to various art media for students with disabilities. Primary focus is on appreciation, tools, techniques and production of individual art projects. May include instruction in hand-formed or wheel thrown ceramic pieces.

ART102

Introduction to crafts involving wood, metal, fibers, ceramics, glass, etc. Make items that are both decorative and utilitarian.

ART103

Learn about the exciting world of Makeup Artistry and the essential techniques to work on yourself and others.

ART104

Focus on color, line, and design. All levels welcome. Application for retail trade, long-arm quilting business and art sales.

Basic

Computer Basics

This course introduces the student to computer concepts and terminology, as well as software and hardware components. Students will develop a basic understanding of how computer systems operate by surveying current computer software applications and their functionality in today's society.

Intermediate

Cisco CCNA

This course presents fundamentals in networking and internetworking structure and theory, IP addressing, Binary Mathematics, LAN topologies and architecture, basic network design, cables and cabling standards, and basic network cabling. Students will learn through theory and hands-on application.

Advanced

Android App Dev

Building and publishing an Android app has never been easier. This course will teach you how to take your app from concept to the app store. Students will take their HTML5 based Android apps and publish them to Google Play and the amazon appstore. Topics include creating app assets, writing a store listing, and publicizing the app.
